Fitness and metrics for Pacman code optimization:

- score: points earned by eating food, completing objectives, and high-value actions.
- survival time: how long Pacman stays alive.
- steps: number of moves taken (fewer is better).

Fitness formula:
fitness = w_score * score + w_survival * survival_time - w_steps * steps

Weights should sum to 1.0.

What good means:
- high score
- long survival
- fewer steps

Example:
- A candidate with strong food collection and good ghost avoidance is good.
- A candidate with many steps but low score is not good.
- A candidate that survives longer and keeps steps low is best.

Use this to rank candidates and choose the best mutated code.
