Pacman strategies and game-playing behavior:

- chase food when the path is safe
- avoid ghosts first, then seek food
- move toward the nearest food pellet
- use safe exploration if no immediate food is nearby
- follow a path that balances survival and score
- prefer ghost-free corridors when possible
- use conditional logic for ghost distance and food distance
- prefer longer survival over risky food hunting when ghosts are close

Example strategy:
if ghost nearby:
    return "run_away"
elif food nearby:
    return "eat_food"
else:
    return "move_randomly"

General game-playing styles:
- reactive: respond to current state only
- cautious: avoid danger first
- greedy: prioritize immediate food
- heuristic-driven: use distance estimates and weights
